Unfortunately, no direct comparison is available between Agili-C and standard microfracture procedures for older patients. The current data only provides indirect insights into the effectiveness of restoration techniques in osteochondritis dissecans treatment.

**Conclusion**

While some clinical trial data suggests that restoration techniques may offer better outcomes, more research is needed to determine the efficacy of Agili-C compared to standard microfracture procedures specifically for older patients.
